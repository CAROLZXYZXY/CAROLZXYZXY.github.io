Hello World

Greetings! I am Carol Xinyi Zheng, an undergraduate strudent student at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science Engineering and Mathematics (Algorithmic Track). I am honorable to be in a dual Bachelor's program from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, a prestigious university in China.
My research interests lie in Intelligent Information Systems, Natural Language Processing and Computational Social Science. Currently, I am a member of Michigan Database Group, working on question answering system over knowledge base. I am also a member of Language and Information Technologies Group, working on project LEAP. Thanks my advisors Prof. Mihalcea and Prof. Jagadish for opening the door of research for me.
In the future, I want to research on understanding human's interaction with information systems and developing new technologies for better human experience. I view Natural Language Processing and the SOTA deep learning as a tool, instead of a research objective. I also cannot refuse to research for social good. Here are some of my dream research topics in PhD Study (if I could be admitted to a PhD program): task-oriented dialogue system, human-in-the-loop of text analytics system, knowledge base construction and application.
