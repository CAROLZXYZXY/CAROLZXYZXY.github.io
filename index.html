Recent Post: Here is the summer of Ann Arbor, what's next?

5/2/2018

It is summer now. The breeze touches my cheeks, I finally realize that there is no more snow this academic year. One of my phD friends told me that, the reason we say the past semester is winter semester is that it is probable to snow every day of that semester. It seems magical, once the semester ends, the temperature began to rise to the most comfortable rate. I love the weather now.

During the really stressful winter semester, I have thought of various ways of spending this summer, either intern, research or go back home. Honestly, I wasted huge amounts of time finding intern when I got stuck in finding research. Every week, several interviews restricted my time schedule. Finally I found that it was all in vain even if I got an offer. Going to work, oh it isn't what I want. Ten years ago, I had a chance to visit Stanford University, fifteen years ago someone called me "Dr.". I know what I want, I always long for universities and knowledge. I decide to stay for Ann Arbor for the whole four months, doing two research in NLP. What's next? Go to research, enjoy the summer, learn to talk to myself, treasure every minute and BE HAPPY!

I saw one of my TAs in SJTU has a headline on his Linkedin: I commit to lifelong study. I agree. I commit to lifelong study. Cheer up for the wonderful summer!
